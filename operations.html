What the phone line misses, the system catches.

Six optional capabilities this website could grow into. Every one of them is a proposal for the owner to accept, change or skip. None of them is active today.

Missed-call text-back Proposed

When a call goes unanswered mid-job, the caller gets a text with a link to the repair request form, so the lead is captured instead of lost to the next shop.

Structured breakdown ticket Proposed

Every form submission becomes a ticket with unit, location, safety status and problem details already filled in before anyone picks up the phone.

Repair-status texts Proposed

Short standard updates at the moments customers actually call about: diagnosed, parts ordered, in the bay, ready for pickup.

Estimate authorization Proposed

Send the estimate by text, get a recorded yes or no, and keep a timestamped approval trail instead of a verbal okay.

Fleet maintenance reminders Proposed

Scheduled service reminders per unit, so fleet work arrives planned during the week instead of broken down on a Friday.

Automated review request Proposed

After pickup, a single polite text asking the customer to share a review, sent once and never nagging.

Optional proposed capabilities, shown for owner review. Nothing on this page is live.
