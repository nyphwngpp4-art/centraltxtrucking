Questions drivers ask

What kinds of trucks and trailers do you work on?

The shop works on heavy trucks and a wide range of trailers, along with diesel engine and electrical work. Call 325-643-6492 to confirm your specific unit before you bring it in.

Do you work with fleets?

Yes. The shop handles work for fleets as well as owner-operators. Call the shop to talk through what your fleet needs.

Can I send my location and photos?

You can include your location in the repair request form on this page, including an optional GPS reading from your phone.

Photo upload is not available yet. If photos would help, mention it when the shop calls back.

Do you perform welding or fabrication?

Welding and metal fabrication work are part of what the shop does. Call with the specifics of your project to confirm scope and timing.

What information should I provide before calling?

Have the unit type, make, model and year, where the unit is, whether it can move, any fault codes, and a short description of the problem. The repair request form above walks through the same details.

What are your current hours?

Posted hours are Monday through Friday, 8 AM to 5:30 PM, closed weekends. Call 325-643-6492 to confirm current hours before you head over.

Do you provide mobile or roadside service?

Yes. Mobile and roadside repair are available, subject to technician availability and service area. For an immediate roadside need, call 325-643-6492.
